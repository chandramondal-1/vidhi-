Our Story

Passion for Perfect Spaces

Founded in 2014, VIDHI INTERIORS was born from a simple belief: every person deserves a space that truly reflects who they are. What began as a small studio in Bankura has grown into one of West Bengal's most celebrated interior design firms.

Our team of 25+ designers, architects, and craftspeople bring together decades of combined experience, a relentless pursuit of perfection, and an innate understanding of what makes a space feel truly alive.
